Crystal Collector


Instructions


You will be given a random target number at the start of the game.


Four crystals appear on the right. If you click on a crystal, your total score increases by the amount of points assigned to that crystal.


You win the game by matching your total score to the target number. If you exceed the target number, you lose.


The value of each crystal will remain hidden. Figure out the value of each crystal by clicking and monitoring your score.


Each time the game restarts, the target number and crystal values will be randomized.


Good luck!
