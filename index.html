Crystal Collector


Instructions


You will be given a random target score at the start of the game.


Four crystals appear on the right. If you click on a crystal, your score increases by the amount of points assigned to that crystal.


You win the game by matching your score to the target score. If you exceed the target score, you will lose.


The value of each crystal will remain hidden. Figure out the value of each crystal by clicking and monitoring your score.


Each time the game restarts, the target score and crystal values will be randomized.


Good luck!

Click the crystals and match the target score!
